Projects

I've spent most of my free time the last few years working on things that interest me. Here are some of them.

Spotify Follower Tracker

Standalone Web App that tracks and monitors the amount of followers your Spotify account has. Emails upon change of this number.

Grade Book Tracker

What started as an interesting class project soon turned into a full fledged grade-calculating system, able to quantify data in many ways through nested dictionaries.

Golden Spiral in Minecraft

I coded a plugin for Minecraft that creates a logarithmic spiral with roughly the growth factor of the golden ratio at the point of clicking.

2D Platformer

Currently working on a 2D platformer designed with tilemapping, spritesheets, and matter-based physics.
